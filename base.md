# 🎬 RFF — Brand Guideline (v1, for fast execution)

## 1. Core Positioning

**RFF = a cinematic female gaze studio.**
Not about films → about **what women feel, reclaim, and express through cinema.**

* **Tone:** intimate, poetic, radical
* **Energy:** quiet power → sudden rupture
* **Duality:** softness × rebellion

👉 Think:

> “A whisper that becomes a scream.”

---

## 2. Emotional Territory

### Primary emotions

* Liberation (breaking invisible constraints)
* Intimacy (internal monologue, close to skin)
* Tension (something unsaid, unresolved)
* Sisterhood (connection, raw bonds)

### Secondary layer

* Fragility → turning into strength
* Silence → turning into voice

---

## 3. Visual Identity Direction

## A. Shapes & Layout (VERY important)

**Everything should feel circular / organic / non-linear**

* Circles, arcs, soft frames
* Avoid rigid grids → use **floating compositions**
* Cropping faces, bodies → partial framing
* Use negative space as tension

👉 Visual metaphor:

> “Orbit / womb / loop / cycle / inner world”

---

## B. Color System

### Base palette

* Deep black (cinema, intimacy)
* Off-white / warm ivory (skin, softness)

### Accent colors (use sparingly)

* Blood red → emotion / rupture
* Dusty rose → feminine but not cliché
* Muted gold → memory / legacy

👉 Avoid:

* Bright saturated colors
* Tech / startup vibes

---

## C. Typography

### Primary font

* Elegant serif (editorial, cinema, auteur)
  → contrast strokes (Didot / Canela vibe)

### Secondary

* Minimal sans-serif (clean, almost invisible)

### Rules

* Large titles, lots of breathing space
* Use **lowercase** for intimacy
* Occasional uppercase for statements

---

## 4. Image Direction

## A. Photography / Frames

* Close-ups (skin, eyes, hands)
* Imperfect / grainy / film texture
* Natural light, shadows
* Slight blur / motion → life, not perfection

## B. Subjects

* Women in **internal moments**
* Not posing → existing
* Not smiling → feeling

👉 Reference vibe:

* Cannes indie cinema
* A24 feminine films
* European auteur cinema

---

## 5. Motion & Interaction (Website)

This is where you can REALLY create differentiation.

### Principles

* Slow transitions
* Organic movement (not linear easing)
* Slight delays → tension

### Examples

* Text fading like breath
* Images revealing in circular masks
* Scroll = emotional progression (not sections)

---

## 6. Copywriting Voice

### Tone

* Minimal
* Poetic
* Suggestive (never explicit)

### Rules

* Short sentences
* No corporate language
* No explanation → evoke instead

### Examples

Instead of:

> “We produce films centered on female narratives”

Say:

> “stories that refuse to stay quiet”
